Pizza

Description

An old classic pizza recipe, even the dumbest could do it

Ingredients

  • 1 g of Dry yeast
  • 600ml of Water
  • 1 kg of Flour
  • 200 ml of Tomatoes sauce
  • 1 kg of Cheese
  • 5 g of Salt

Steps

  1. Add 1 g of yeast to 600ml of water and mix
  2. Add the flour and mix until it's homogeneous
  3. let it rest for a day
  4. Make 6 small balls
  5. Put it on a sheet pan, add tomato sauce and cheese and cook it for 10 minutes
  6. Slice and serve
